About us

We are an independent organisation committed to protecting the NHS from defunding and privatisation. We do so by making official data regarding the performance and resourcing of the NHS as accessible as possible and distributing this information to the public.

We are not affiliated with the NHS in any way. However, we make use of official data published by the NHS itself.

All of our code is publically available. If you want to reproduce some of our graphs, or if you just want to check what we're doing, our code can be found on GitHub:

  • Data collection
  • Graph plotting and website generation
If you have any questions or want to inquire about how you can get involved, please email us at inquiries@howsmynhs.co.uk.
