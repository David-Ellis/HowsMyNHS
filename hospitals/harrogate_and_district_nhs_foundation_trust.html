How's my NHS?

Harrogate And District NHS Foundation Trust

After nearly a decade of Conservative rule, on average, 315 more people are being left to wait over four hours at A&E at your hospital than back in 2010.

And things are bad for the rest of England too. Each month over 400,000 more people are made to wait over four hours to be seen at A&E than in 2011.

Now, Brexit and a trade deal with Trumps US threatens to increase the NHS drug bill from £18 billion to as much as £45 billion a year while shutting out the nurses, carers and other workers that the health service depends on.
